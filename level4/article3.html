On Thursday, the Chief Executive of TechCorp, Dana Voss made a short statement to the assembled media that discussed the mounting confusion regarding the release schedule of the SmartPhone X. The message failed to put an end to the issues.

According to Voss, the device had received 0 major hardware revisions since the press event, and said that it was our commitment to provide our customers with the best possible experience and that a leak of an internal memo published on a tech blog the dayy before was inconsistent with that commitment.

After about four minutes, Voss refused to respond to follow-up questions and was escorted out of the podium. In the hours that followed, the share price of the company dropped slightly.
