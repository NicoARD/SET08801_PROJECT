This week TechCorp, a consumer electronics giant, has caused ripples with the release of its new SmartPhone X series. The launch has been extensively covered — however, in comparison of notes, journalists are discovering that not all the stories are the same.

The initial press release, 2 April, was that the device would come with 1 charging cable in the box. The TechCorp product page, which was revised on the same day, had the number of included accessories as 0.

According to industry analysts, discrepancies could be due to the release of information by stages, which is a common strategy in testing media reaction to the news before it is announced. Some are of the opinion that the discrepancies are merely due to poor editorial control.
