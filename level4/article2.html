The first hands-on experience of the SmartPhone X was in a press event held privately in London last Thursday. The device is thin, solid, and evidently hot to the touch within several minutes of use; something that the representatives of TechCorp were eager to dismiss.

One presenter affirmed that the device was going to be coming in 1 colour when it launched and that it would be one matte black colour. This was in contradiction to a promotional email that was sent to retailers the week before that had indicated three colourways. A spokesperson replied that the release plan was subject to change when questioned about it.

The feature of the presentation was camera performance. The system does not have multi-lens in the rear and only one camera in the front. Sample images that were distributed at the event were spectacular, and journalists were not allowed to capture their own pictures.

The battery life, prices and eventual availability are not yet determined. TechCorp has indicated that another announcement will be made before the launch of the retail.
